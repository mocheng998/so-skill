## Product Development Timeline

### Typical Product Development Cycle (POD Products)

**Week 1: Sourcing and Validation**
- Use the Three Consumption Moments framework to identify opportunities
- Use eRank and Google Trends to validate demand
- Competitor analysis and pricing research

**Week 2: Design and Production**
- Design products (self or outsourced)
- Create products on Printify
- Place test order for samples

**Week 3: Listing and Optimization**
- Write title, description, tags
- Take or create product photos
- Publish to Etsy shop

**Week 4: Promotion and Monitoring**
- Social media promotion
- Monitor data and feedback
- Optimize based on data

**Total**: Approximately 4 weeks from sourcing to listing

**Recommendation**: Allow at least 8-12 weeks (2-3 months) for development and promotion of seasonal/holiday products.

## Risk Management

### Common Risks and Response Strategies

**Risk 1: Missing the Time Window**
- **Prevention**: Strictly follow the schedule, set reminders
- **Response**: If missed, pivot to the next timeline

**Risk 2: Seasonal Product Oversupply**
- **Prevention**: Validate market demand in advance, test in small batches
- **Response**: Timely markdown for clearance, or hold for next year

**Risk 3: Design and Production Delays**
- **Prevention**: Allow buffer time, choose reliable suppliers
- **Response**: Communicate with customers, provide compensation

**Risk 4: Trend Misjudgment**
- **Prevention**: Use data to validate, avoid blind following
- **Response**: Adjust quickly, pivot to other products

## Usage Instructions

1. **Plan at Year Start**: Fill in the complete annual plan at the beginning of each year
2. **Monthly Check**: At the start of each month, review the month's action items to ensure timely execution
3. **Quarterly Review**: Conduct review and adjustment at the end of each quarter
4. **Flexible Adjustment**: Adjust plans flexibly based on actual circumstances; don't execute rigidly
5. **Data-driven**: Adjust strategy based on actual sales data, not subjective judgment

**Recommendation**: Print this template or save as electronic document, review and update regularly.
