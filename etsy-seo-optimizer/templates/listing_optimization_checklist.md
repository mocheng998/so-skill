# Etsy Listing Optimization Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure your Etsy listing is optimized according to best practices.

## 📋 Keyword Research

- Conduct keyword research using eRank or other tools
- Identify 1-2 "superstar keywords" (high search volume)
- Identify 3-5 "low-hanging fruit" keywords (medium search volume + low competition)
- Identify 5-8 long-tail keywords (low search volume + low competition)
- Record search volume and competition data for each keyword

## 📝 Title Optimization

- Title length is close to the 140-character limit (fully utilize the space)
- Place core keywords at the front of the title (first 20-30 characters)
- Include at least 1 complete "superstar keyword" phrase (exact match)
- Include 2-3 "low-hanging fruit" keywords
- Include 1-2 long-tail keywords
- Title reads naturally; avoid keyword stuffing
- Use separators (such as "|" or "-") to improve readability
- Title accurately describes the product (do not mislead buyers)

## 🏷️ Tag Optimization

- Use all 13 tags
- First 5-7 tags repeat core keywords from the title
- Last 6-8 tags add relevant terms not included in the title
- Each tag contains complete keyword phrases (do not split)
- Include product attribute tags (such as "handmade," "custom," "vintage")
- Include use and scenario tags (such as "gift," "wedding," "home decor")
- Avoid unrelated or overly broad tags

## 📸 Product Images

- Upload at least 5 high-quality images
- Main image clearly displays the product subject (white or clean background)
- Include product detail images (material, size, craftsmanship)
- Include lifestyle images (product in actual use context)
- Include size reference images (compared with common objects)
- Images have sufficient lighting and accurate colors
- Image size is at least 2000x2000 pixels (enables zoom feature)

## 📄 Product Description

- Description is detailed and informative (at least 200 words)
- Opening paragraph includes core keywords
- Clearly state product material, size, color, and craftsmanship
- Describe product use and applicable scenarios
- Include purchase and usage instructions (such as customization process, care methods)
- State shipping time and policy
- Use paragraphs and lists to improve readability
- Tone is friendly and professional; avoid aggressive marketing

## 💰 Pricing and Inventory

- Price is competitive (reference similar products)
- Price accurately reflects product value and cost
- Inventory quantity is accurate (avoid overselling)
- Offer multiple variant options (such as size, color)
- Variant prices are set reasonably

## 📦 Shipping and Policies

- Set accurate shipping time (processing time + transit time)
- Offer multiple shipping options (standard, expedited)
- Shipping policy is clear and explicit
- Return and exchange policy is buyer-friendly and meets Etsy requirements
- Shop policies are complete (privacy, customization, cancellation, etc.)

## 🔍 Categories and Attributes

- Select the most accurate product category
- Fill in all relevant product attributes (material, color, size, etc.)
- Attribute information is consistent with title and description
- Select correct "Who Made It" and "What Is It" options

## 📊 Post-Launch Monitoring (60-90 days)

- Regularly check listing views and click-through rate
- Monitor favorites and cart additions
- Track conversion rate and sales
- Avoid frequently changing title and tags during the observation period
- Focus on optimizing images, description, and customer service

## 🔄 Continuous Optimization (After 90 days)

- Use eRank or Etsy Stats to analyze keyword performance
- Identify underperforming keywords and replace them
- Test new keyword opportunities
- Change only one variable at a time (title or tags)
- Record changes and dates for easy performance tracking
- Regularly (monthly) review and update keyword strategy
- Pay attention to seasonal trends and market changes

## ✅ Final Check

- All information is accurate
- Keywords in title, tags, and description are consistent and cohesive
- Listing page is appealing to buyers
- Complies with all Etsy policies and rules
- Ready to provide excellent customer service

---

**Tip**: Save this checklist and use it each time you publish or optimize a listing to ensure you don't miss any important steps.
