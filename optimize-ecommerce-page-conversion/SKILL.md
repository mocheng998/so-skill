# Optimize Ecommerce Page Conversion

Optimize Shopify store pages (Home, Collection, PDP, Promo) using conversion benchmarks and industry checklists to maximize store revenue.

## When to Use
- When store traffic is consistent but conversion rate (CR) is below industry benchmarks.
- Before launching major marketing campaigns to ensure the landing page is optimized.
- When high bounce rates are observed on specific page types (e.g., product pages or collections).

## Industry Benchmarks
- **Average E-commerce CR:** 2.0% - 4.0%.
- **Mobile vs. Desktop:** Desktop typically converts 1.5x - 2x higher than mobile; focus on mobile-first design.
- **Above-the-Fold Visibility:** Hero section must communicate value within 3 seconds.
- **Cart-to-Checkout Rate:** Target > 50%.

## Page-Specific Frameworks

### 1. Home Page (Brand Trust & Navigation)
- **Objective:** Establish credibility and direct traffic to high-intent collection/product pages.
- **Checklist:**
  - Clear value proposition in the hero header.
  - Social proof (as seen in, trust badges, star ratings) above the fold.
  - High-quality lifestyle imagery showcasing products in use.
  - "Shop Now" CTAs that link directly to top-selling categories.

### 2. Collection Pages (Discovery & Filtering)
- **Objective:** Help customers find the right product as quickly as possible.
- **Checklist:**
  - Dynamic filtering (Size, Color, Price, Availability).
  - High-quality, consistent product photography (e.g., all white backgrounds).
  - Quick-add to cart functionality for low-consideration items.
  - Secondary hover images (e.g., model view vs. product view).

### 3. Product Detail Pages (PDP) - The Conversion Engine
- **Objective:** Answer all objections and drive the "Add to Cart" action.
- **Checklist:**
  - **Main Image:** The highest impact factor. Use zoomable, high-res images and 360-degree views if possible.
  - **Add to Cart (ATC) Button:** High contrast, sticky on mobile.
  - **Social Proof:** Quantity and quality of reviews (aim for 4.5+ stars).
  - **Urgency/Scarcity:** "Limited stock" or "Selling fast" alerts based on real inventory.
  - **Value Stack:** Clear shipping info, return policy, and installment payment options (e.g., Shop Pay) near the ATC button.

### 4. Promotional Landing Pages (Campaign Focus)
- **Objective:** Single-focus conversion for ads or email campaigns.
- **Checklist:**
  - Message match between ad copy and page headline.
  - Minimal navigation (remove main menu to reduce exits).
  - Tiered discount visuals (e.g., Buy 2 Get 1 Free).
  - Countdown timers for time-limited offers.

## A/B Testing & Experimentation Ideas
- **Test 1:** Image Type - Lifestyle vs. Studio shots on PDP hero.
- **Test 2:** CTA Wording - "Add to Cart" vs. "Buy Now" vs. "Get Yours Today".
- **Test 3:** Social Proof Placement - Reviews below ATC vs. Reviews below product description.
- **Test 4:** Free Shipping Threshold - Testing $50 vs. $75 threshold impact on AOV vs. CR.
