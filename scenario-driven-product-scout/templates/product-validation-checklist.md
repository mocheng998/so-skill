### 6.2 Success Metrics

- Views: _______
- Favorites: _______
- Sales: _______

**Week 2 Targets**:
- Views: _______
- Favorites: _______
- Sales: _______

**Success Criteria** (meet at least 2 of the following):
- At least 1 sale in first 2 weeks
- Favorites > 5% of views
- Sustained growth in views

**Failure Criteria** (meet any 1 of the following):
- Views < 50 within 2 weeks
- No favorites or sales within 2 weeks
- High negative review or return rate

### 6.3 Iteration Plan

- Expand product variants (color, size)
- Develop related product lines
- Increase marketing investment

**If Test Fails**:
- Optimize keywords and photos
- Adjust pricing
- If no improvement, consider delisting

## Usage Instructions

1. **Complete All Sections**: Do not skip any section; each provides important information
2. **Data-driven**: Fill in based on tool data, not subjective guesses
3. **Honest Assessment**: If the product doesn't meet standards, be willing to abandon it
4. **Keep Records**: Save each validation checklist to build a product sourcing database
5. **Regular Review**: Review checklists after testing to summarize lessons learned

**Recommendation**: Fill out one checklist per product idea, then compare and select the best product to enter.
