# Ad Platform Product Research Methods

## TikTok Creative Center

### Access Method
- URL: https://ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter/
- No login required to view partial data

### How to Use

1. **View Trending Ads**
   - Go to "Top Ads" section
   - Filter by: region, industry, time range
   - Focus on ads with high likes, comments, and shares

2. **Analyze Ad Creative**
   - Video creative formats (UGC, scenario display, comparison, etc.)
   - Copy structure and selling point extraction
   - Visual elements and editing style
   - CTA (Call-to-Action) design

3. **Track Landing Pages**
   - Click ad to view landing page
   - Analyze product pricing strategy
   - Study page conversion elements
   - Record product supplier information

4. **Identify Trending Products**
   - Multiple accounts running similar products in a short time
   - Rapid growth in ad engagement metrics
   - Novel product features or use cases

## Facebook Ad Library

### Access Method
- URL: https://www.facebook.com/ads/library/
- Can view all active ads without login

### How to Use

1. **Search Strategy**
   - Search by keyword (product name, category)
   - Search by advertiser (competitor brands)
   - Filter ad type (e-commerce, apps, etc.)

2. **Ad Analysis Dimensions**
   - Ad run duration (long-term running indicates good performance)
   - Number of ad variations (multiple variations indicate emphasis)
   - Ad copy and visual style
   - Target audience positioning clues

3. **Competitor Monitoring**
   - Build competitor ad monitoring list
   - Regularly check competitors' new ads
   - Analyze competitor product line expansion direction
   - Learn commonalities of successful ads

## Other Ad Platforms

### Google Ads Transparency Center
- URL: https://adstransparency.google.com/
- View Google display ads and YouTube ads

### Pinterest Ads
- Search for product keywords on Pinterest
- Look for content marked with "Promoted"
- Suitable for home, fashion, and handicraft products

## Product Research Workflow

1. **Daily Monitoring** (15-30 minutes)
   - Browse TikTok Creative Center trending ads
   - Record 3-5 potential products

2. **Deep Analysis** (Weekly)
   - Select 2-3 products for in-depth research
   - Analyze ad strategies of multiple competitors
   - Evaluate market competition level

3. **Data Recording**
   - Product name and images
   - Advertiser information
   - Ad creative links
   - Landing page URL
   - Preliminary assessment (market potential, competition level, profit margin)

## Notes

- Ad spend does not equal product success; combine with other data for validation
- Pay attention to ad run duration; long-term running usually indicates positive ROI
- Avoid direct copying; understand the underlying marketing logic
- Combine with Google Trends to validate product trend momentum
