# E-commerce Platform Product Research Methods

## Amazon

### Best Sellers Ranking

Access path: Amazon homepage → Best Sellers → Select category

**Usage Strategy**

The Best Sellers ranking provides a quick overview of top-selling products across categories. Focus on products ranking in the top 100, as they typically have stable market demand. When analyzing, pay attention to the number of reviews and ratings—a high review count (1000+) indicates sustained sales, while a high rating (4.5+) indicates product quality is recognized.

**Analysis Dimensions**
- Product price range: Understand the price range acceptable to consumers
- Review quantity and quality: Identify product improvement opportunities through negative reviews
- Product variants: Popularity of options such as color and size
- Brand concentration: Assess whether the market is dominated by major brands

### Movers & Shakers

Access path: Amazon homepage → Movers & Shakers

This ranking displays products with the fastest-rising rankings and is an important tool for discovering trending products. If a product's ranking rises significantly within 24 hours, it may indicate the product is becoming a hot trend. Quickly verify whether this is a short-term spike or a long-term trend.

### New Releases

Access path: Amazon homepage → New Releases

The new releases ranking helps discover innovation directions in the market. Focus on products that receive a large number of reviews soon after listing—this usually indicates either strong market promotion or a product that truly meets unmet demand.

## AliExpress

### Access Methods
- Homepage bestseller list
- "Orders" sorting on category pages
- "Find Similar" feature to track product variants

### Product Research Methods

AliExpress is an important source of product inspiration and supplier discovery. Sorting by "Orders" reveals actual sales data, which is more valuable than page views alone. Focus on products with order volumes of 1000+ and ratings above 4.5.

**Supplier Evaluation**
- Store rating and establishment time
- Product image quality (assess whether dropshipping is supported)
- Shipping time and logistics options
- Whether customization is supported (brand labels, packaging, etc.)

**Price Analysis**
Calculate the potential profit margin of products. Generally, a reasonable retail price range is 2.5-4 times the AliExpress price. Factor in shipping costs, advertising costs, platform commissions, and other expenses.

## eBay

### Use Cases
Suitable for discovering niche products and secondhand market opportunities

**Trending on eBay**
eBay regularly publishes trend reports showing keywords and categories with the fastest-growing search volume. This data is valuable for understanding market dynamics.

**Sold Listings Analysis**
Review sold item prices and sales velocity to assess real market demand for products. Filter by "Sold Items" and sort by "Recently Listed" to see actual transaction activity.

## Etsy

### Suitable Categories
Handmade items, personalized customization, artwork, home decor

### Product Research Methods

Etsy search results can be sorted by "Bestseller"—these products typically have unique designs and higher profit margins. Focus on niche markets with high sales but few competitors.

**Creative Inspiration**
Etsy products often have unique designs and positioning that can serve as inspiration for product differentiation. Even if not selling on Etsy, these ideas can be applied to other platforms.

## Product Research Workflow

### Weekly Routine Research (1-2 hours)

First, visit Amazon Best Sellers and Movers & Shakers, browse 3-5 categories of interest, and record the top 20 products in each. Then search for these products on AliExpress to evaluate supply chain feasibility and price margins. Finally, conduct supplementary research on eBay and Etsy to find differentiation angles.

### In-Depth Product Validation

After selecting 2-3 potential products, conduct in-depth analysis. Read Amazon product reviews, especially 3-star and 4-star reviews, to understand product strengths and weaknesses. Contact 2-3 suppliers on AliExpress to inquire about MOQ (minimum order quantity), customization options, and sample prices. Use Google Trends to verify search trends for product keywords.

### Data Recording Template

Create a record for each product researched, including: product name and category, Amazon ranking and review data, AliExpress supplier information and prices, estimated retail price and profit margin, market competition assessment, and product differentiation opportunities.

## Important Notes

Platform data only reflects conditions on that specific platform—cross-platform validation is needed. Best-selling products may not suit all sellers; consider your own resources and capabilities. Pay attention to seasonal characteristics of products to avoid entering the market at the wrong time. Be cautious of products that appear very popular but have minimal profit margins—low-price competition is not sustainable.
